Department of Computer Science

Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering & Technology
Head of Department : Dr. Birmohan Singh
Office phone : 01672-253121, 01672-253122
Office fax : 01672-253121

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering started in 1991. Global excellence and local relevance in teaching, research, and technology development is the main objective of the department. The Department is imparting quality education at the level of Integrated Certificate and Diploma (ICD) in Computer Science and Engineering, B.E. (Computer Science & Engineering), M.Tech. (Computer Science and Engineering) and Ph.D. Today the Department has a vibrant student body numbering about 700. Students of postgraduate and Ph.D. are working on various advanced area of research and mostly students are supported by scholarships. The department also offers several attractive facilities for outstanding Ph.D. scholars and liberally supports participation in top-ranking international conferences. Department’s efforts towards the students provide a solid foundation in core and emerging areas of computer science and trains students to become independent researchers. Every year, the department produces very reputed research publications in top-tier conferences and journals. The department has a star-studded alumni’s, consisting of leading researchers around the world, entrepreneurs, experts, successful engineers and impactful policy makers. The Department is well equipped with high end computers, latest software & state-of-the-art IT infrastructure and all these computing resources are inter-connected with high speed intranet. Our students are exposed to up-to-date curriculum, technology and techniques. The Department has well experienced & dedicated faculty members with different specializations. The department also organizes expert talk by eminent personalities working in the field of Computer Science & Engineering periodically. The Department prides itself on good career opportunities for students. Our students graduate with good placement through campus. Many companies of repute show their interest to visit our Institute for campus recruitment.
